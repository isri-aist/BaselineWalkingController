Detailed Description

DDP problem of CoM-ZMP model.

Dynamics is expressed by the following equation.

\begin{align*} \begin{bmatrix} \ddot{c}_x \\ \ddot{c}_y \\ \ddot{c}_z \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \dfrac{(c_x - z_x) f_z}{m c_z} \\ \dfrac{(c_y - z_y) f_z}{m c_z} \\ \dfrac{f_z}{m} - g \end{bmatrix} \end{align*}

\(\boldsymbol{c}\) and \(\boldsymbol{z}\) are CoM and ZMP.

State and control input are expressed by the following equations.

\begin{align*} \boldsymbol{x} = \begin{bmatrix} c_x \\ \dot{c}_x \\ c_y \\ \dot{c}_y \\ c_z \\ \dot{c}_z \end{bmatrix}, \boldsymbol{u} = \begin{bmatrix} p_x \\ p_y \\ f_z \end{bmatrix} \end{align*}
